Pole pole Safari

Duration: 4 Days 3 Nights

Routes: Tsavo West, Amboseli, Chyulu, Devils Lava, Tsavo East, Mombasa


Itinerary


This tour starts after you arrive in Nairobi in the morning.


Day 1


Depart for Tsavo West National Park by Tsavo River gate. Safari en route for lunch and installation at Ngulia Safari Lodge and rest until 4:00pm. Depart for the late afternoon game drive until Sundown.


Day 2


Depart after breakfast for Amboseli National Park through Devils Lava. Lunch and installation at Kibo Safari Camp located at the foot of Kilimanjaro. Depart for a late afternoon game drive until Sundown.


Day 3


Depart after breakfast for Tsavo East National Park for Lunch and installation at Voi Safari lodge.


Day 4


After breakfast, depart for a game drive until the exit. Safari en route to Mombasa arriving around 1:00pm at the Hotel you booked.
