Pride Of Kenya

Duration: 13 Days 12 Nights

Routes: Nairobi, Samburu, Mount Kenya, Lake Nakuru, Lake Elementaita, Maasai Mara, Lake Naivasha, Amboseli, Tsavo East, Mombasa.


Itinerary


The Safari starts after you arrive in Nairobi


Day 1


Reception at the Airport the depart for Samburu National Reserve. Safari en route lunch and full Accomodation at Samburu Simba Lodge, game drive in the late afternoon untill Sundown.


Day 2


After breakfast, game drive in the reserve, return to the lodge for lunch, rest until late afternoon for an additional evening game drive until sundown, possibility of visiting the Samburu Village and Turkana Village (Optional)


Day 3


After an early breakfast, depart at around 0730hrs for Mount Kenya. Installation at Misty Mountain lodge. Afternoon free with an Optional walk around the Lodge, discovery of the highly altitude, primary forest and observations of birds and animals.


Day 4


After an early breakfast, depart for Lake Nakuru National Park. Game drive in the park, Lunch and accommodation at Lake Nakuru Lodge. In the late afternoon at around 4:00pm, leave for a game drive until Sundown.


Day 5


Game drive in the Lake Nakuru National Park. Later installation at Lake Elementaita Countr lodge at around 12:30pm for lunch and then free time to admire the beautiful around the Lake Elementaita.


Day 6


After breakfast, depart for the Famous Maasai Mara National Reserve lunch and installationat the tented camp of Enkorok Mara Camp.rest and leave for a late afternoon game drive until Sundown.


Day 7


After breakfast, Safari all day with picnic lunch Return to the camp at around 4:00pm wit the possibility of visiting the Maasai Village (Optional)


Day 8


Depart for Lake Naivasha, Lunch and accommodation Naivasha Simba Lodge. At the end of the afternoon choose a Boat ride trip to approach hippopotamuses and many water birds or free afternoon.


Day 9


Depart for Amboseli National Park lunch and installation at Kilima Safari Camp. In the late afternoon game drive in the park at the foot of Kilimanjaro until Sundown.


Day 10


Full day Safari in Amboseli National Park with picnic lunch. Return to the Camp around 3:30pm.


Day 11


After breakfast, depart for Tsavo East National Park lunch and installation at Voi Safari lodge. Game drive in the late afternoon until Sundown.


Day 12


After breakfast, depart for a game drive en route to the exit, and then depart for Mombasa at the Hotel of your choice.
